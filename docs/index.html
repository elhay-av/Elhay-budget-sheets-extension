Installation Guide

1

Download the Extension

Click the download button above to get the latest .zip file.

2

Unzip the Package

Extract the downloaded ZIP to a folder on your computer.

3

Open Extensions Page

Navigate to chrome://extensions in your browser and enable Developer mode (top-right toggle).

4

Load Unpacked

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder. The Finanda Export Bridge icon will appear in your toolbar.

5

Open Google Sheets

Open your Finanda-connected Google Sheet and launch the sidebar — the extension will automatically detect and connect.
